# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Project Sentinel is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and safety-focused collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critique on ideas and implementation, not individuals.
- Avoid discriminatory, harassing, or abusive conduct.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberate disruption of community processes.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, warn contributors, or restrict participation for conduct violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns to project maintainers through repository issue templates or designated maintainer channels.
